Know your signs

Road safety & warning signs

Every learner must recognise these signs before getting behind the wheel.

Stop
Come to a complete stop, then proceed only when safe.
Give Way
Slow down and let other traffic pass before proceeding.
Pedestrian Crossing
Watch for people crossing and be ready to stop.
Speed Limit
Maximum speed allowed on that stretch of road, in km/h.
Slippery Road
Reduce speed — road surface may be wet or slick.
No Entry
Entry into this road or lane is strictly prohibited.
School Zone
Slow down — children may be crossing nearby.
Roundabout
Give way to traffic already circulating, then merge in.
