Everything you need to know about training at Dekay — from enrolment to your DVLA test day.

Enrolment
How do I sign up to Dekay Driving School?
Call or WhatsApp us on 0274 211 566 to get started. We'll book a consultation, discuss the right course for you and create your student account. You can also fill in our contact form and we'll call you back.
What documents do I need to register?
You'll need a valid Ghana card or passport, a passport-size photo (or we can take one at the school), and proof of address. For commercial licences (bus, truck), you'll also need your existing car licence.
Is there an age requirement?
Yes. You must be at least 18 years old to enrol in any driving course, in line with DVLA Ghana regulations. There is no upper age limit.
Can I start at any time or are there fixed intake dates?
We run a rolling intake — you can begin as soon as your registration is complete and your first payment is made. No waiting for a new cohort to start.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), and bank transfer. A deposit is required to secure your spot. Speak to us about instalment options if needed.
Courses
Which course should I choose — manual or automatic?
If you want flexibility to drive any car, including older Ghanaian vehicles, choose Manual. If you mainly drive modern cars in the city, Automatic is quicker and easier. Note: an automatic licence restricts you to automatic vehicles only. When in doubt, go manual.
How many lessons are in each course?
The number of lessons depends on your course type and how quickly you progress. Most students attend 3 sessions per week. Sessions are typically 1–1.5 hours each. Our instructors will let you know when you're ready to move to the next stage.
Do I need any driving experience before joining?
No experience needed for any of our personal courses (manual, automatic, motorcycle). For commercial courses (bus, truck), you must hold a valid car licence first, as required by DVLA Ghana.
What vehicles will I train in?
Our fleet includes saloon cars for manual and automatic training, motorcycles for the bike course, and appropriate commercial vehicles for bus and truck trainees. All vehicles are regularly serviced and insured.
Can I switch courses once I've started?
Yes, in most cases. Speak to our admin team and we'll advise on any price difference or additional requirements. Switching from automatic to manual will require starting practical lessons from the beginning.
Student Portal
What can I do in the student portal?
Your portal gives you access to: theory video lessons, online quizzes with instant scores, your attendance log, progress tracking through the 6 training stages, notifications from your instructor, and your profile and training status.
How do I log in to the student portal?
Visit our login page and enter your email and password. Your login credentials are created when you register. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password" link on the login page to reset it via email.
Can I watch the theory videos on my phone?
Yes — the portal is fully mobile-friendly. Watch lessons, complete quizzes, and check your attendance from any smartphone, tablet, or computer. You just need an internet connection.
Will the school see how much of each video I've watched?
Yes. The portal tracks your video completion percentage for each lesson (e.g., "watched 70%"). This helps your instructor know which topics to revisit with you in practical sessions. It's not about catching you out — it's about making sure no learning gaps are missed.
Attendance
How do I mark my own attendance?
On your lesson day, log into your student portal and tap "Mark Attendance" from your dashboard. This opens a check-in form that's only active during scheduled session hours. The school can see your check-in in real time. Your instructor will also confirm it during the session.
What if I miss a session?
Absences are logged automatically if you don't check in. Please call or WhatsApp us ahead of time if you can't make a session. Our admin team can reschedule missed lessons — though this may extend your overall course duration depending on availability.
Can I see my full attendance history?
Yes. Your Attendance tab in the student portal shows a full log of every session — date, status (present / absent), and which stage you were at. Your instructor and admin team can see the same log.
DVLA & Licence
When will I be ready for my DVLA test?
Your instructor will advance you to "DVLA Ready" status in your portal after you complete practical lessons and pass the internal mock test. Most students reach this stage by the end of their course duration. We won't send you to DVLA until we're confident you'll pass.
Do you go with students to the DVLA test?
Yes. One of our instructors accompanies every student on their DVLA test day. We know the examiners and the common routes — this alone makes a significant difference to pass rates. This escort is included in your course fee.
What happens if I fail the DVLA test?
We'll review what went wrong and offer refresher sessions to address any gaps. The DVLA requires a waiting period before you can re-sit. Additional sessions after a failed test may be charged separately — speak to us and we'll work out the best plan.
How long does it take to get my licence after passing?
After passing, DVLA Ghana typically issues a provisional licence receipt on the same day, and the physical licence within a few weeks. Processing times can vary. We'll advise you on the exact DVLA steps and documentation needed during your test day escort.
