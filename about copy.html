Our story

From one car to Accra's most trusted school

Dekay Driving School started with a single vehicle and one simple belief: that every Ghanaian deserves patient, professional driving instruction — not rushed lessons and crossed fingers at the DVLA gate.

What began at Ablekuma NIC Junction as a small operation quickly grew into one of Accra's most trusted driving programmes. Word spread not because of advertising, but because our graduates passed. First time. Every time.

Today we offer five licence types, a digital student portal with video lessons and quizzes, and we accompany every student to their DVLA test day. The mission hasn't changed — only the scale.

Ablekuma NIC Junction

Our home base — central, accessible from Kaneshie, Dansoman, and all of central Accra.

DVLA-certified instructors

Every instructor holds a current DVLA certification and has taught on Accra roads for at least 3 years.

Digital student portal

Theory videos, quizzes, attendance tracking and progress monitoring — all on your phone.

We go to DVLA with you

Every student is escorted on their test day by one of our instructors. This is included — no extra charge.

What we stand for

Our values

Everything we do at Dekay comes back to these three commitments.

01
Patience above everything

Learning to drive is stressful. Our instructors are trained to meet every student where they are — no rushing, no judgment, no shortcuts.

02
Results you can measure

A 98% first-time pass rate is not a marketing claim. It is the outcome of a structured programme, proper preparation, and an honest assessment of when a student is truly ready.

The people

Meet our team

Mr. Kwaku Adom Obiri Antwi
Founder & Head Instructor

Mr. Kwaku Adom Obiri Antwi is a distinguished professional driving instructor with over 12 years of experience training drivers on Accra's roads. He founded Dekay Driving School at Ablekuma NIC Junction with a clear mission: to raise the standard of driver education in Ghana by combining patience, structure, and genuine investment in every student's success.

Under his leadership, the school has grown from a single vehicle to a full-service driving academy offering five licence types, a digital student portal, and a 98% first-time DVLA pass rate. Mr. Antwi personally accompanies every student to their DVLA test day — a commitment that reflects his belief that the instructor's job does not end in the car park.

Our journey

Key milestones

How Dekay grew from one car at Ablekuma into a full-service driving school.

2016
Dekay Driving School founded

Mr. Kwaku Adom Obiri Antwi opens the school at Ablekuma NIC Junction with one vehicle and a simple vision: patient, professional driver training for every Ghanaian.

2018
Commercial licence programme launched

Growing demand from logistics and transport workers leads to the introduction of bus and truck training, with Emmanuel Agyei joining as commercial specialist.

2020
100th graduate licensed

A milestone year — our 100th student receives their DVLA licence. The 98% first-time pass rate is established as a school benchmark.

2023
Motorcycle programme & fleet expansion

We add motorcycle training and expand the vehicle fleet. Kwabena Mensah joins as dedicated motorcycle instructor.

2026
Digital student portal launched

Dekay goes digital — online video lessons, quizzes, attendance tracking, and a full student progress dashboard accessible from any phone.
